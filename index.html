About Pogiso's Tours

Founded in 2010, Pogiso's Tours began with a simple vision: to revolutionize the chauffeur service industry by combining professional excellence with cutting-edge technology. What started as a small operation with five vehicles has grown into a trusted transportation provider serving thousands of clients across the region. Our founder, Pogiso Mahlare, built the company on the principle that every passenger deserves a premium experience, regardless of whether they're traveling for business or pleasure. Over the past decade, we've invested heavily in our fleet, technology infrastructure, and team training to ensure we remain at the forefront of luxury transportation services.

Today, our mission is clear: to provide safe, reliable, and luxurious transportation that exceeds expectations while maintaining the highest standards of professionalism and integrity. We believe in putting our customers first, which is why we've implemented real-time tracking, transparent pricing, and 24/7 customer support. Our core values—excellence, reliability, professionalism, and customer-centricity—guide every decision we make. We're committed to continuous improvement, regularly updating our fleet with the latest models and ensuring our drivers receive ongoing training in customer service and safety. At Pogiso's Tours, you're not just booking a ride; you're joining a family of satisfied customers who trust us with their most important journeys.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services, booking process, and pricing.

How do I book a ride with Pogiso's Tours?

Booking with us is incredibly simple and can be done in just a few steps. You can book through our mobile app (available on iOS and Android), our website at pogisostours.co.za, or by calling our customer service team at any time. Simply enter your pickup location, destination, preferred time, and vehicle type. Our system will instantly show you the estimated fare and available drivers in your area. Once you confirm your booking, you'll receive a confirmation message with your driver's details and real-time tracking information. You can also schedule rides in advance for future dates, making it perfect for regular business trips or planned events.

What is your pricing structure and are there any hidden fees?

We believe in complete transparency when it comes to pricing. Our rates are calculated based on distance, time, and current demand, and you'll see the exact fare estimate before you book. All prices include taxes and service charges—there are absolutely no hidden fees. What you see is what you pay. For corporate clients, we offer customized pricing packages based on volume and frequency of bookings. Loyalty members also enjoy exclusive discounts and special rates. We provide detailed invoices and billing statements for all rides, making it easy to track expenses for business purposes. If you ever have questions about a charge, our customer service team is always available to explain and assist.

Can I track my driver in real-time?

Absolutely! One of our signature features is our advanced real-time GPS tracking system. Once you book a ride, you'll have access to a live map showing your driver's exact location, their route, and an accurate estimated arrival time. You can share this tracking information with colleagues, clients, or family members by simply sending them a link. The tracking updates every few seconds, so you always know exactly where your driver is. This feature provides peace of mind and helps you plan your schedule more effectively. You can access tracking through our mobile app or website from any device, at any time during your ride.

Do you offer corporate or bulk booking discounts?

Yes, we absolutely do! We specialize in corporate transportation solutions tailored to your organization's needs. Whether you need regular employee transportation, client pickups, or event logistics, we can create a customized package that offers significant savings compared to standard pricing. Our corporate accounts include benefits such as centralized billing, detailed reporting and analytics, dedicated account management, priority driver allocation, and flexible payment terms. We work with companies of all sizes, from small startups to large enterprises. To discuss a corporate package or bulk booking discount, please contact our corporate sales team at info@pogisostours.co.za or visit our website to request a quote. We'll be happy to create a solution that fits your budget and requirements.

What should I do if I have an issue with my ride or driver?

Your satisfaction is our top priority, and we take all feedback seriously. If you experience any issues with your ride or driver, please report it immediately through our app or website. You can also contact our customer support team at info@pogisostours.co.za or call us directly. Our team will investigate the matter promptly and take appropriate action. We have a comprehensive quality assurance program in place to ensure our drivers maintain the highest standards of professionalism and service. If you're not satisfied with the resolution, we offer a full refund guarantee. We also encourage you to leave feedback after each ride, which helps us continuously improve our service and maintain our commitment to excellence.
